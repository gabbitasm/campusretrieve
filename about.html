About Campus Retrieve

Operational core directives and information security measures handling property recovery processes.

Campus Retrieve is a dedicated platform designed to streamline the lost and found process for students, faculty, and visitors at the BYU-Idaho campus.

Our goal is simple: to provide a centralized, reliable method for reporting missing property and ensuring found items reach the correct administrative recovery points safely and efficiently.

Please note: High-worth items such as wallets, phones, and official identification cards are prioritized and handled with strict validation procedures to protect student privacy and security.

If you have any questions, operational feedback, or need direct assistance regarding an item you have tracked, please use the Contact link below to reach our team.
